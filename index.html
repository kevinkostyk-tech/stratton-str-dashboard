Winhall Short-Term Rental Ordinance (Effective Dec 4, 2024)
**Applies to:** Any property rented for fewer than 30 consecutive days and for more than 14 days per calendar year.

Key Licensing & Registration Rules

✅
License Required
Must be obtained prior to advertising or renting.
💰
Annual Fees
$500 per bedroom. Minimum fee: $1,000. Maximum fee: $4,000 annually.
🔄
Renewal
Licenses are valid for one year from issuance and must be renewed annually.

Operating Requirements

📋
Display Requirements
STR license and visitor emergency information must be posted conspicuously.
🔥
Fire Safety
Comply with Vermont Fire & Building Code. Self-certification for ≤ 8 persons; formal inspection for ≥ 9.
🛡️
Insurance
Proof of commercial liability insurance required.
🅿️
Parking & Site Plan
Site plan showing designated parking must be submitted.

Taxes and Compliance

💸
Lodgers’ Tax (Winhall)
10% tax on gross short-term rents. Owners are responsible for manual remittance via the Winhall GovOS portal.
📈
Vermont State Surcharge
3% surcharge (as of Aug 1, 2024) in addition to existing 9% Rooms Tax (Total VT: 12%).
🚨
Fines
Properties not in compliance are subject to daily fines.
