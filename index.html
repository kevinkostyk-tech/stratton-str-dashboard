Ordinance effective Dec 4, 2024. Applies to any property rented for fewer than 30 consecutive days AND more than 14 days per calendar year. No grandfather clause for existing rentals — all must license. Platform auto-collection does NOT cover Winhall's 10% local tax; you remit manually.

💳 Licensing

🔑
License required before advertising or renting
💰
$500 per bedroom / year
Minimum $1,000 · Maximum $4,000
📅
Annual renewal required
Runs 1 year from issue date
📋
Application checklist includes site plan + parking diagram, insurance proof, safety worksheet

💸 Tax Obligations

🏛️
Winhall Lodgers' Tax: 10%
On gross rents. Manual remittance required — Airbnb/VRBO do NOT auto-collect this.
🏔️
Vermont Rooms Tax: 9%
State-level. Platforms (Airbnb/VRBO) DO collect and remit this one.
📈
VT STR Surcharge: 3%
New as of Aug 1, 2024. In addition to 9% Rooms Tax.
🔢
Total effective tax: 22% of gross rents
On every dollar of revenue earned.
💻
Winhall remittance portal: winhallvt.munirevs.com

🔒 Operating Requirements

📌
License + emergency info posted inside unit at all times
🔥
Vermont Fire & Building Code compliance required
≤8 occupants: self-certification worksheet. ≥9 occupants: formal DFS inspection.
🛡️
Commercial liability insurance required
Must submit with application. Standard homeowner's policy usually insufficient.
🚗
Site plan with designated parking required
Drawn to scale. Submitted with application.

Daily fines for non-compliance
