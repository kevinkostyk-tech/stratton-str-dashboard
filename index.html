💸 Tax Obligations

🏛️
Winhall Lodgers' Tax: 10%
On gross rents. Manual remittance required — Airbnb/VRBO do NOT auto-collect this.
🏔️
Vermont Rooms Tax: 9%
State-level. Platforms (Airbnb/VRBO) DO collect and remit this one.
📈
VT STR Surcharge: 3%
New as of Aug 1, 2024. In addition to 9% Rooms Tax.
🔢
Total effective tax: 22% of gross rents
On every dollar of revenue earned.

Winhall remittance portal: winhallvt.munirevs.com
