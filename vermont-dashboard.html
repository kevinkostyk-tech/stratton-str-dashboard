What to Ask Listing Agents
  1. Does the property have an active STR permit or rental history? Can you share revenue numbers (Airbnb / VRBO dashboard)?
  2. Is the property in Winhall township? Any HOA or deed restrictions on short-term rental?
  3. What is the current DOM and has the seller received any prior offers?
  4. Has the seller indicated motivation / flexibility on price?
  5. Are there any known deferred maintenance items or system ages (roof, HVAC, water heater)?
  6. Is there a hot tub? Is it operational and included in sale?
  7. What is the road maintenance situation — town-maintained or private?

Why Stratton / Winhall
🎿 Ikon Pass — Unlimited Access
PASS MATCH
Stratton is one of the few Vermont resorts on Ikon with unlimited days (vs. Killington which is capped at 7). For a family buying Ikon passes at ~$5,177/yr, ownership near Stratton maximizes pass value and eliminates day tickets entirely.
🏔️ Multi-Mountain Access
LOCATION
Winhall sits at the center of Southern VT skiing. Stratton: 7 min. Bromley: 12 min. Magic Mountain: 18 min. Renters get options — major STR differentiator over single-mountain locations.
📋 STR Regulatory Environment
FAVORABLE
Vermont does not currently have statewide STR restrictions. Winhall township is permit-friendly. VT imposes meals & rooms tax (~9%) on STR income. Confirm no HOA restrictions at specific property. Market has robust Airbnb/VRBO demand in ski season.
