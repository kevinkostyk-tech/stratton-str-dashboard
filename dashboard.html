Verdict

Chamonix needs no introduction — Mont Blanc, the Vallée Blanche, year-round climbing and hiking. But at €12,000/m² average, a 70m² apartment costs €840K. SFH chalets start at €1.5M+. The market has risen 40%+ over the past decade. Your budget buys a studio or tiny apartment here. Listed for completeness because of its iconic status, but not a viable investment market at $850K for any meaningful property. Noted: if you find a deal below market or a distressed property, the rental rates are exceptional.
